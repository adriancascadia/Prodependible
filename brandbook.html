The Risks of Working with Lesser-Known Contractors

Homeowners who choose unlicensed or inexperienced contractors face significant risks:

  • Liability Exposure: Without proper insurance, homeowners may be liable for injuries or property damage
  • No Recourse: Unlicensed contractors often disappear when problems arise, leaving homeowners with no legal recourse
  • Code Violations: Improper work can result in failed inspections, fines, and costly corrections
  • Poor Workmanship: Inexperienced contractors may lack the skills to deliver quality results
  • Project Abandonment: Financially unstable contractors may abandon projects mid-completion

Dependable Home Improvement eliminates these risks. Our established reputation, proper licensing, comprehensive insurance, and 20+ year track record provide peace of mind that your home improvement investment is protected.
